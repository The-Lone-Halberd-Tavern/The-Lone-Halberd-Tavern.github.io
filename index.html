Quick Skillet

  • Time:35 mins
  • Serves:8

Basic needed equipment

  1. stove/oven
  2. skillet or Saute pan
  3. Spattula




Ingredients Quantity
ground Beef 2lb
Rice-A-Roni Pilaf 2 boxes
butter 4 tbs.
water 4 cups

Step 1:In large skill brown ground beef. Drain and set aside.

Step 2:In same skillet prepare Rice-A-Roni stopping at end of step 2 on box.

Step 3:Mix ground beef into the Rice-A-Roni and countinue with instuction on box.

Step 4:Serve and injoy.

Sheperds Pie

  • Time:30 mins
  • Serves:6

Basic needed equipment

  1. stove/oven
  2. skillet or Saute pan
  3. Spattula
  4. 9x13 pan





Ingredients Quantity
ground Beef 2lb
4 cheese instant mashed potatos 2 bags
great value frozen peas and carrots mix. 1 bag
instant gravy 2 packets
grated mexi-blend cheese 4 cups

Step 1:In large Saute Pan brown ground beef. Drain and return to pan.

Step 2:In the skillet with the ground Beef prepare gravey following instructions on the packet.

Step 3:Once Gravy starts to thicken add frozen peas and carrots mix.

Step 4:Pour mixture into 9x13 pan.

Step 5:Prepare mashed potatos folowning instruction on bag and layer atop mixture in the 9x13 pan.

Step 6:Turn broiler on oven to high

Step 7:Sprinkle cheese atop mashed potatoes

Step 8:Put 9x13 pan into oven for 5 minute or until chease begins to turn a golden brown

Step :Take out of oven cool for 5 minutes serve and injloy

Stuffed Bell Peppers

  • Serves:4

basic needed equipment

  1. Stove/oven
  2. Skillet or Saute pan
  3. Spattula
  4. Can opener
  5. Cutting board
  6. Sheat pan
  7. Knife





Ingredients Quantity
ground Beef 1lb
Bell Pepper 4
Taco seasoning packet 1.
tomato paste 8 oz can
black beans 1 can

Step 1:Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit

Step 2:In large skill brown ground beef. Drain and return to pan.

Step 3: Add taco seasoning and follow packet instructions

Step 4:Add black eans and tomtopaste simmer for 15 mins.

Step 5:Split peppers and core them.fill with mixture .

Step 6: place in oven on baking sheet for 15 mins or untill pepper look cooked

Step 7:Serve and injoy
